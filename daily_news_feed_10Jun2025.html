Image not found.Aditya Birla’s realty arm onboards IFC for residential projects - VCCircle

Published Date: Mon, 09 Jun 2025

Birla Estates, the real estate arm of the Aditya Birla Group, has secured a $50 million investment from the International Finance Corporation (IFC) for two residential projects in Pune and Thane. The IFC investment will be in the form of rupee-denominated non-convertible debentures, giving it a 44% economic interest in the projects. These projects, developed through separate special purpose vehicles, will encompass 6,000 to 9,000 housing units with an overall cost of approximately $1 billion. This marks IFC's second investment in an Aditya Birla Group company this year, following a $96 million investment in Aditya Birla Housing Finance Ltd. IFC has been actively investing in India's residential real estate sector, partnering with developers like Sunteck Realty to promote affordable housing. Birla Estates, established in 2016, has expanded its portfolio beyond Mumbai's commercial real estate to include residential and commercial developments across key Indian markets.
