India's retail inflation rose to 2.5% in August 2025, up from 2.0% in July, driven by increased prices of vegetables, fruits, and cereals. By supporting a wider range of African issuers in accessing global capital markets, DFIs can contribute to poverty reduction and sustainable economic development in SSA.
