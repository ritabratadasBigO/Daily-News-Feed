Image not found.FICO CEO defends credit score pricing amid FHFA criticism, says cost isn't blocking home ownership - CNBC

Published Date: Thu, 31 Jul 2025

Fair Isaac (FICO) CEO Will Lansing defended the company's pricing practices for credit scores, arguing that the cost is minimal compared to overall mortgage expenses. Lansing's comments come after Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) head Bill Pulte criticized FICO's price hikes and allowed lenders to use alternative credit scores from VantageScore. Pulte called FICO a "monopoly" that has "ripped off Americans for decades." Lansing countered that FICO's new credit score model, FICO 10T, outperforms both the classic FICO model and VantageScore, and that FICO holds a dominant market share in most sectors. He also emphasized that FICO has been competing with VantageScore for 15 years and consistently wins. The FHFA's decision to allow the use of VantageScore has sparked debate about the potential impact on the safety and soundness of the mortgage market.

Image not found.Bajaj Allianz General Insurance, Tata AIG, and United India join CRED Garage as insurance partners - The Financial World

Published Date: Thu, 31 Jul 2025

CRED, a fintech platform, has expanded its motor insurance offerings on its platform, CRED garage, by adding Bajaj Allianz General Insurance, Tata AIG, and United India Insurance. This brings the total number of curated insurance providers on the platform to seven, allowing members to compare and choose from leading Indian insurers. CRED garage has facilitated the insurance of over 1 million vehicles, enabling seamless policy management, including comparisons, quotes, renewals, and digital claims initiation. The platform leverages members' creditworthiness to offer dynamically priced premiums, with better rates for those with higher credit scores. Insurers view this partnership as a way to reach tech-savvy, financially responsible consumers, while CRED aims to provide a frictionless and transparent insurance experience for its members. CRED garage also offers a range of other car ownership services, including challan discovery, pollution certificate renewal, valuation checks, and resale assistance.

Image not found.Rabobank Forecast: Aggressive Fed Rate Cuts In 2026, EUR/USD Target 1.20 - Exchange Rates Org UK

Published Date: Thu, 31 Jul 2025

The Euro to Dollar exchange rate (EURUSD) has fallen to a five-week low below 1.09, prompting Rabobank to predict a firm dollar tone over the next 13 months. The bank anticipates the EURUSD will remain under pressure in the short term due to a new EU-US trade agreement imposing 15% tariffs on European exports. Despite this, Rabobank expects the EURUSD to strengthen to 1.15 by Spring 2026, driven by anticipated four interest rate cuts by the Federal Reserve in 2026. The bank believes the European Central Bank will maintain its current deposit rate of 3.75%, while the Fed's rate cuts will erode the dollar's yield advantage. Rabobank had previously anticipated a correction from the EURUSD's 45-month highs reached in early July, citing a substantial buildup in long Euro positions. Overall, the bank sees a mixed outlook for the EURUSD, with short-term weakness followed by potential appreciation in 2026.
