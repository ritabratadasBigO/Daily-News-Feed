This transaction sets a precedent for future foreign acquisitions in India's banking sector, potentially encouraging further foreign investment with higher voting rights caps.

Image not found.Fixed Income Weekly: July Rate Cut Gathers Momentum - DBS

Published Date: Thu, 26 Jun 2025

DBS Bank India has launched DBS Treasures, a premium banking and wealth management program tailored for high-net-worth individuals. The program offers personalized banking, wealth management, lifestyle, travel, and health privileges based on a tiered system based on Total Relationship Value (TRV). TRV is calculated by aggregating average quarterly balances across various accounts, including savings, term deposits, insurance, investments, and loans. Customers with a TRV of INR 30 Lacs to INR 1 Cr enjoy a range of privileges, while those with higher TRVs (INR 1 Cr to INR 2 Cr and INR 2 Cr to INR 6 Cr) gain access to even more exclusive benefits. DBS Treasures also provides access to market insights, bespoke lifestyle and wellness privileges, and a dedicated customer support team. The program aims to elevate the banking experience for discerning clients seeking comprehensive financial solutions and personalized service.

Published Date: Thu, 26 Jun 2025

Dutch investor sentiment towards the United States has declined significantly under President Trump's leadership. Two-thirds of Dutch investors in American companies have halted further investments, with a quarter reducing existing holdings due to concerns about Trump's policies and rhetoric.
