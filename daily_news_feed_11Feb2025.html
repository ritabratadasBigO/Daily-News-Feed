Chinese New Year Bank Notes 2025, Check OCBC, DBS, My Bank Reservation & Notes Collection Dates - Pasteur Institute of India

Published Date: Mon, 10 Feb 2025

In Singapore, giving new notes during Chinese New Year (CNY) is a cherished tradition. To make this practice convenient and sustainable, major banks like DBS, OCBC, and My Bank have implemented various measures. These include online reservations for collecting new notes, expanded ATM services, and digital gifting options like DBS's QR Gift and OCBC's eAng Bao. Over 65% of angbao givers opted for fit notes (reusable, high-quality notes) in 2024, highlighting the growing popularity of this eco-friendly alternative. Banks also offer walk-in exchanges for customers who prefer not to use online reservations, prioritizing senior citizens and people with disabilities. These initiatives ensure a smooth and inclusive CNY experience for everyone while promoting sustainability.

The National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) has ordered the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) to formally withdraw its insolvency plea against Byju's parent company, Think & Learn Private Limited. Earlier, the NCLAT had directed the NCLT to expedite its decision on the withdrawal application. Concerns were raised during the hearing regarding the conduct of the Resolution Professional, Pankaj Srivastava, who was accused of delaying CoC meetings. The NCLT has asked the parties involved to file an appropriate application to address these concerns.
