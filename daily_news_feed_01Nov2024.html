Image not found.Stocks Under F&O Ban: IDFC First Bank, Indiamart Intermesh, RBL Bank. and Others - EquityPandit

Published Date: Thu, 31 Oct 2024

The National Stock Exchange (NSE) banned four stocks from trading in the futures and options (F&O) segment on October 31 due to high open market interest. The banned stocks are IDFC First Bank, Indiamart Intermesh, Punjab National Bank, and RBL Bank, as their open interest exceeded 95% of the marketwide position limit (MWPL). The ban will be lifted once the position falls below 80%. Traders who buy or sell these securities will be penalized, but they can still trade in the cash market. The NSE updates the F&O ban list daily, serving as a guide for traders and investors.
