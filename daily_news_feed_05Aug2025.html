Image not found.IRDAI slaps Rs 5 crore penalty on Policy bazaar for various violations - Greater Kashmir

Published Date: Mon, 04 Aug 2025

The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) has imposed a ₹5 crore penalty on Policybazaar for violating several provisions of the Insurance Act and IRDAI regulations. The violations include key managerial personnel holding directorships in other companies without prior IRDAI approval and allegedly promoting insurance products in a biased manner. Policybazaar was also found guilty of ranking insurance products as "top" or "best" without providing sufficient information to consumers for informed decision-making. Additionally, the IRDAI found that Policybazaar's website domain name was owned by its holding company, raising concerns about compliance with IWA regulations. Policybazaar has acknowledged the violations and stated that current key personnel no longer hold directorships in other companies. The company has 45 days to remit the penalty amount.
