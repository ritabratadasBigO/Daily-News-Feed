Image not found.FICO's BNPL Credit Score Revolution: Opportunity or Risk for Affirm, Klarna, and the Future of Consumer Finance? - AInvest

Published Date: Sat, 05 Jul 2025

FICO's upcoming BNPL-integrated credit scores, launching in fall 2025, will significantly impact the Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) industry. These new scores will factor BNPL payment history into creditworthiness, potentially boosting credit scores for timely payers and expanding access to credit for those with limited history. Affirm, which has already been reporting BNPL data to credit bureaus, is well-positioned to benefit from this shift, potentially attracting more users and driving loyalty. However, Klarna, which has only partially reported BNPL data, faces challenges as its unreported short-term loans could expose it to increased scrutiny and potential reputational damage. The inclusion of BNPL data in credit scores introduces both opportunities and risks for BNPL platforms, with those demonstrating robust data sharing practices and low delinquency rates likely to thrive. Investors should closely monitor the performance of BNPL companies in this evolving landscape, considering factors such as data reporting transparency and underwriting discipline. Ultimately, the success of BNPL platforms will depend on their ability to navigate the new credit scoring paradigm and demonstrate responsible lending practices.
