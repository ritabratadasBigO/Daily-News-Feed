Google Cloud has significantly expanded its commitment to enterprise AI in Singapore, unveiling new innovations and partnerships to accelerate the nation's AI ambitions. Image not found.21 years after officer went missing, HC directs Indian Bank to clear dues and offer job to family - madhyamamonline.com

Published Date: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

The Telangana High Court has ruled in favor of Vanapatla Sugunakumari, whose husband, an Indian Bank officer, went missing in 2004. The court determined that under the Indian Evidence Act, a person missing for over seven years is legally presumed dead, granting Sugunakumari's request for her husband's terminal benefits. Justice Nagesh Bheemapaka also ordered the bank to provide a compassionate appointment to one of Sugunakumari's children based on their qualifications within eight weeks. The court's decision came after years of struggle for Sugunakumari and her family, who faced delays and denials from the bank despite a police report confirming her husband's disappearance and a legal heir certificate. Additionally, the court imposed a fine of Rs 50,000 on the Director of Sainik Welfare for failing to respond to the family's appeals. This case highlights the importance of legal recourse for families facing similar situations and the need for financial institutions to process such claims promptly and fairly.

Image not found.Amid geopolitical turbulences, can BRICS be bridge for South - The Hans India

Published Date: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

BRICS, initially an economic acronym coined by Goldman Sachs, has evolved into a significant global forum with expanded membership and influence. The group, formally established in 2006, aims to provide alternatives to debt-ridden economies through people-centric and non-conditional platforms. Brazil and India, key members, view BRICS as a platform for convergence and delivery, focusing on climate finance, digital trade, and energy security. However, China and Russia often frame BRICS as a counterweight to the West, promoting platforms like the New Development Bank and emphasizing anti-Western rhetoric. Despite these differing perspectives, BRICS' credibility hinges on tangible outcomes, including extending finance without stringent conditions, fostering trade, and amplifying the Global South's priorities. Calls for institutionalization, such as the establishment of a BRICS Secretariat, highlight the group's growing ambition and potential impact.

Image not found.Aditya Birla Capital injects ₹250 crore into home finance arm - BusinessLine

Published Date: Sun, 31 Aug 2025

Aditya Birla Capital Ltd (ABCL) invested ₹250 crore in its wholly-owned subsidiary, Aditya Birla Housing Finance Ltd (ABHFL), through a rights issue. This investment aims to support ABHFL's growth and enhance its leverage ratio. ABHFL's loan portfolio expanded by 70% year-on-year to ₹34,605 crore as of June 2025, driven by balanced growth in both prime and affordable segments. ABHFL's gross NPAs improved from 1.5% in June 2024 to 1.3% in June 2025, demonstrating a positive trend in asset quality. The company's funding mix shifted, with NCDs increasing to 45% and term loans decreasing to 31% in Q1FY26, leading to a 11 bps reduction in the cost of borrowing. ABHFL passed on a 15 bps rate cut to retail customers, effective July 2025, reflecting the benefits of lower borrowing costs.
