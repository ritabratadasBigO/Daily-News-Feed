Published Date: Wed, 23 Jul 2025

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) has announced that lenders will be allowed to use VantageScore, a second credit scoring methodology, alongside the dominant FICO Score for government-sponsored mortgages. This decision, while positive for VantageScore, caused a dip in FICO's stock price. The FHFA has been working towards expanding credit scoring models since 2014, aiming to provide more options and potentially reduce costs. Lenders will be required to deliver loans with both VantageScore and FICO 10T scores after a transition period. While not all credit scoring models incorporate factors like rent payments and Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) lending, recent advancements aim to address these gaps and provide a more comprehensive view of an individual's financial health. FICO is also incorporating BNPL data into its scoring algorithms, recognizing its growing prevalence among consumers. This shift towards more inclusive credit scoring models could benefit consumers with limited traditional credit history.

DBS Bank India Limited has launched DBS Treasures, a premium banking and wealth management program tailored for high-net-worth individuals. The program offers personalized banking, wealth management, lifestyle, travel, and health privileges based on the customer's Total Relationship Value (TRV) with the bank. TRV is calculated by aggregating average quarterly savings account balances, term deposits, insurance, investments, and mortgage loan outstanding. DBS Treasures provides exclusive benefits such as signature lifestyle privileges, exclusive offers, unlimited ATM withdrawals, and the lowest forex markup on international spends. The program also features a new invite-only, pure metal card and a digital banking platform for managing finances and wealth. Customers with a TRV of INR 30 Lacs or more are eligible to join DBS Treasures.

Image not found.LIC Signs MoU with Ministry of Rural Development to expand Bima Sakhi Scheme - Elets BFSI

Published Date: Wed, 23 Jul 2025

Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) has partnered with the Ministry of Rural Development to expand the Bima Sakhi Yojana, a program aimed at empowering rural women through financial literacy and insurance awareness. The scheme provides training and stipends to women aged 18-70 who have completed at least 10th grade, equipping them to become LIC insurance agents. Upon completion of the three-year training, Bima Sakhis receive commissions based on their performance and can progress to higher roles within LIC. The initiative, launched in December 2024, aligns with the government's goal of financial inclusion and social security, targeting 100,000 participants. By educating local communities about insurance benefits, Bima Sakhis aim to bridge the insurance penetration gap in rural areas. This collaboration leverages the Ministry's network to promote government insurance schemes like PMJJBY and PMSBY among vulnerable populations.

Image not found.Funding U Student Loans: 2025 Review - CNBC

Published Date: Thu, 24 Jul 2025

Funding U is a student loan provider that focuses on academic performance rather than credit history. They offer fixed interest rates, no cosigner requirements, and flexible repayment terms. Loan amounts range from $3,001 to $20,000 per academic year, with interest rates varying based on GPA, school year, and state of residence. Funding U also offers several advantages, including no application, origination, or prepayment fees, and the ability to make small monthly payments while in school. However, loans are only available to full-time undergraduate students and cannot be used for past school years, summer classes, or fully online programs. Other student loan options, such as Ascent and MPower Financing, offer larger loan amounts, more flexible repayment terms, and cater to a wider range of students, including graduate students and international students.
