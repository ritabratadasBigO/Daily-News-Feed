Image not found.Slovakia to stop blocking new EU sanctions on Russia - DW

Published Date: Fri, 18 Jul 2025

Slovakia has ended its blockade of the 18th EU sanctions package against Russia, paving the way for its approval on Friday. The blockade stemmed from concerns over a separate EU proposal to phase out gas imports from Russia by 2028, which Slovakia addressed through guarantees from the EU regarding gas prices and supply. The sanctions package targets Russia's energy and banking sectors, prohibiting EU operators from using the Nord Stream pipelines and decreasing the oil price cap. It also aims to isolate the Russian financial sector by cutting almost two dozen banks from the SWIFT international payment system and prohibiting transactions with entities facilitating commerce with Russia. The EU seeks to reduce reliance on Russian fossil fuels following the invasion of Ukraine, with Germany already ending direct imports of Russian gas in 2022. The sanctions package represents a continued effort to pressure Russia economically.
