FICO will launch two new credit scores in the fall that incorporate buy now, pay later (BNPL) data. These scores, FICO Score 10 BNPL and FICO Score 10 T BNPL, will be offered alongside existing FICO scores at no additional cost to lenders. The move aims to provide lenders with a more comprehensive view of consumers' repayment behavior, particularly for those who primarily use BNPL products. The integration of BNPL data into credit scores is expected to have a significant impact on the financial services industry, potentially expanding access to credit for underserved populations and providing lenders with more accurate risk assessments.
