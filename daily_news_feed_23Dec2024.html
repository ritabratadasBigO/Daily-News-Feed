Smallcap stocks have seen PE / VC exits worth over 54,000 crore in 2024 - Qrius

Published Date: Mon, 23 Dec 2024

In 2024, private equity and venture capital firms exited their stakes in Indian smallcap companies, with 48% of deals worth 53,900 crore. Midcap companies accounted for 34% of deals, while largecap companies saw deals worth 20,800 crore. The total number and value of deals increased from 2023, with 103 unique companies seeing deals worth 1.65 lakh crore. Out of these companies, 65% are trading above their deal price, with an average gain of 22%. Examples of successful exits include Paytm, whose shares rose over 10% after Ant Fin exited in March 2024, and Prudent Corporate Advisory Services, whose shares rose 140% after TA Associates sold its stake in February. Insider deals, including buying and selling, resulted in net selling of 44,700 crore, with significant selling seen in stocks like IndiGo, ITC, and Whirlpool.
