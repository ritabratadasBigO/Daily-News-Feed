CredAble Enhances Credit Support for MSMEs with CGTMSE Scheme - SMEStreet

Published Date: Tue, 11 Feb 2025

The Ministry of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and SIDBI established the Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) to improve credit access for MSEs. CredAble, a digital lending platform, has joined CGTMSE as a member lending institution, enabling it to offer guaranteed loans to micro and small businesses. Through the CGTMSE Udaan Guarantee Scheme, CredAble's loans are now secured with a 75% guarantee cover, mitigating risk and expanding credit availability. This partnership allows CredAble to deepen its wallet share and provide short-term working capital loans to underserved segments, particularly in supply chain financing. CredAble aims to increase its lending to MSEs from the current 30% to 40-45% of its portfolio, further supporting the financial inclusion of these businesses. This move, coupled with government initiatives like the increased credit guarantee cover and revised MSME classification criteria, signifies a positive step towards empowering India's micro and small enterprises.
