Image not found.Stocks Under F&O Ban: RBL Bank, and Others - Equitypandit

Published Date: Mon, 11 Aug 2025

The National Stock Exchange (NSE) has banned trading in the futures and options (F&O) segment of PNB Housing Finance Ltd and RBL Bank. This ban is due to the open market interest for these securities exceeding 95% of the marketwide position limit (MWPL) set by the exchanges. The MWPL restricts the maximum number of contracts that can be opened simultaneously. Traders attempting to buy or sell these securities in the F&O segment will face penalties. However, trading in the cash market for these stocks remains unaffected. The NSE updates this F&O ban list daily to guide traders and investors. The ban will be lifted once the open interest falls below 80% of the MWPL.
