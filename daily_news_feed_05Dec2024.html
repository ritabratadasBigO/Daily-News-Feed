Rabobank has invested in fraud detection fintech ThreatFabric, a software-as-a-service package for bank web and mobile channels. ThreatFabric's Fraud Risk Suite uses behavior-based location intelligence and behavioral biometrics to detect fraud. The company's AI-powered technology defines criminal attack paths and adds risk scores to the overall behavioral analytics engine. Rabobank has been a long-term client of ThreatFabric and has collaborated with the company on its products. The partnership aims to elevate ThreatFabric's products to enterprise-grade standards, with Rabobank's Anton Jan Cozijnsen stating that "cocreation is what makes their product suites so powerful."
