Despite the recent price decline, FICO's future remains uncertain as the adoption of VantageScore gains momentum.

The Cyber Police of the North Region Division apprehended two individuals, Mookbashir Hidayatullah Rahat Kikar and Suraj Khedkar, for their involvement in an online fraud scheme. The duo collaborated with international cybercriminals, primarily based in Cambodia, to facilitate the transfer of fraudulently obtained funds. Kikar lured victims with offers of free holiday packages, collecting their bank account details under the pretense of booking reservations.
