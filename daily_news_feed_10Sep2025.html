Image not found.South Indian Bank launches new digital ‘Loan Against Mutual Funds’ - BusinessLine

Published Date: Tue, 09 Sep 2025

South Indian Bank has launched a new digital loan facility called "Loan Against Mutual Funds" in partnership with Ark Neo Financial Services. This paperless service allows customers to access loans against their mutual fund investments, with funds disbursed quickly and efficiently. The loan application process is fully digital, requiring customers to register, complete KYC verification using their PAN and Aadhaar, and then access funds based on their mutual fund holdings. Individuals aged 18 to 75, both existing and new bank customers, are eligible for this facility. Loan amounts are determined by the type of mutual fund, with up to 50% of equity fund value and up to 70% of debt fund value available as a loan. This initiative aims to provide a convenient and flexible solution for investors to meet their financial needs without disrupting their investment portfolios.

Image not found.ING Netherlands makes another €100 million available for the National Heat Fund - ING.com

Published Date: Tue, 09 Sep 2025

ING Netherlands and Rabobank are each providing an additional €100 million to the National Heat Fund, boosting its lending capacity by €200 million. This will enable the fund to support 12,500 households in making their homes more energy efficient through favorable loans, sometimes even interest-free or with deferred repayments for lower-income households. The banks' contributions, totaling €600 million for ING and €550 million for Rabobank, are part of a broader public-private partnership aimed at tackling rising energy poverty. The National Heat Fund offers a structural solution to help homeowners reduce their energy bills permanently, as opposed to temporary support measures. In 2024, the fund granted a record €426 million in loans, supporting nearly 30,000 homeowners, and has already disbursed €223 million in the first half of 2025. Both banks emphasize their commitment to supporting the energy transition and making sustainability accessible to all homeowners.
