Image not found.PAYING RENT ON TIME - Cape Gazette

Published Date: Wed, 20 Aug 2025

Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac announced a policy change allowing mortgage lenders to use VantageScore credit ratings in addition to or instead of traditional FICO scores. This change expands credit access to borrowers with limited credit history, particularly renters with a history of on-time rent payments. VantageScore considers rent payment history reported to major credit bureaus, unlike some versions of FICO. The policy is expected to boost the housing market by increasing the pool of potential homebuyers. This move could also increase competition in the credit score industry, potentially leading to lower fees and closing costs for consumers. FICO, the creator of the FICO score, welcomes competition but maintains that the FICO score remains the industry standard for evaluating creditworthiness.

DBS Bank India Limited has launched DBS Treasures, a premium banking and wealth management program tailored for high-net-worth individuals. The program offers personalized banking, investment, lifestyle, travel, and health privileges based on a tiered system based on Total Relationship Value (TRV). TRV is calculated by aggregating balances across various accounts, including savings, term deposits, insurance, investments, and mortgages. DBS Treasures provides exclusive benefits such as signature lifestyle privileges, unlimited ATM withdrawals, and a low forex markup on international transactions. The program also offers access to expert insights on Indian and international markets, as well as digital banking tools for managing finances and wealth. To qualify for DBS Treasures, individuals need to maintain a minimum TRV of INR 30 Lacs.
