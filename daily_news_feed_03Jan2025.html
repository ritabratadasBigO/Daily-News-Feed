IDFC FIRST Bank launches IDFC FIRST Academy on eve of New Year 2025 - The Hans India

Published Date: Thu, 02 Jan 2025

IDFC FIRST Bank launched IDFC FIRST Academy, a free online financial literacy program accessible via website and mobile app. The Academy offers bite-sized modules, videos, blogs, and interactive quizzes covering 255 financial topics across three levels (Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced). Courses are designed for diverse learning styles and include real-world scenarios, culminating in certification upon completion. The program's modular structure allows users to start at any level based on their existing knowledge, gradually deepening their understanding. The curriculum progressively explores complex topics like savings and investments, simplifying concepts like mutual funds for beginners. The Academy aims to empower individuals with the knowledge to better manage their finances.

Rajat Verma to take over as DBS Bank India CEO in March 2025 - United News of India

Published Date: Thu, 02 Jan 2025

Rajat Verma, currently Head of Institutional Banking Group (IBG) at DBS Bank India, will succeed Surojit Shome as CEO upon the latter s retirement on February 28, 2025. In this new role, Verma will be part of DBS Group Management Committee. Shome has significantly expanded the India franchise, including through the launch of digibank, India s first mobileonly bank in 2016.
