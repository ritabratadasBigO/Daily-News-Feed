The situation in Slovakia highlights the ongoing struggle to protect media independence and the rule of law in Europe.

Image not found.CBI nabs absconding accused in 8-year-old housing loan fraud case - thehawk.in

Published Date: Fri, 04 Jul 2025

The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) arrested Harsh Sharma, a fugitive for eight years, in connection with a housing loan fraud case involving Punjab National Bank. Sharma was accused of conspiring with builder Ms Shree Balaji Hitech Construction Pvt. Ltd to defraud the bank through a bogus housing loan scheme. The CBI alleged that Sharma supplied fake KYC documents, enabling the bank to disburse a loan based on fabricated transactions involving multiple bogus buyers. A chargesheet was filed against Sharma and others in March 2024, but he evaded court appearances, leading to a non-bailable warrant. After a prolonged search, Sharma was apprehended and remanded to judicial custody until July 15, 2025. The CBI continues to investigate the case for further involvement and wider conspiracy.

Image not found.Haven of Hope – Shelter Home for Underprivileged Cancer Patients Inaugurated in Kochi - The Wire India

Published Date: Fri, 04 Jul 2025

South Indian Bank inaugurated "Haven of Hope," a dedicated shelter home for cancer patients in Kochi, India. The four-story facility, built on land owned by the Sisters of the Destitute congregation, cost over 3 crore rupees and offers accommodation for up to 32 individuals. Chairman V.J. Kurian emphasized the bank's commitment to social impact and highlighted the need for a new "Project for Poor People" (PPP) model to address societal needs. The shelter home is equipped with medical beds, a kitchen, and other necessary facilities to provide a safe and dignified space for patients undergoing treatment. The project was undertaken in memory of Venerable Fr. Varghese Payyappilly, a Catholic priest known for his service to the poor and suffering. Haven of Hope is expected to commence operations soon, offering compassionate care to underprivileged cancer patients.
