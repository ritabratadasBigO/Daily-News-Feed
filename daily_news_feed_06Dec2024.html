Top 7 Best international student loans of 2024 - Businessday

Published Date: Thu, 05 Dec 2024

Securing funding for international students can be challenging due to rising tuition fees and living costs. Many financial organizations offer specialized international student loans, but it's crucial to understand the features, benefits, and drawbacks of each loan product. Earnest stands out for its flexible repayment options and competitive interest rates, but requires a cosigner for most borrowers. Juno pools borrowers together to negotiate competitive rates, but may not offer the best deals elsewhere. Credible simplifies the loan shopping process by allowing students to compare rates from multiple lenders, but terms and rates vary widely. Ascent caters to international students, particularly at the graduate level, but requires a U.S.-based cosigner and doesn't allow cosigner release.
