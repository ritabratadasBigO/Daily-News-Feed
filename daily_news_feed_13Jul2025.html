Image not found.Anthem Biosciences IPO: ₹1,016cr raised from 60 institutional investors - NewsBytes

Published Date: Sat, 12 Jul 2025

Anthem Biosciences, a Bengaluru-based contract research, development, and manufacturing organization (CRDMO), has successfully raised ₹1,000 crore from 60 institutional investors through its anchor book ahead of its initial public offering (IPO). The IPO, which opens on July 14th and closes on July 16th, has a price band of ₹540-570 per share. Notable global investors, including Abu Dhabi Investment Authority and Government Pension Fund Global, participated alongside prominent Indian asset management companies like HDFC Mutual Fund and ICICI Prudential AMC. Insurance firms such as ICICI Prudential Life Insurance and Axis Max Life Insurance also invested in the anchor book. The ₹3,395 crore IPO is entirely an offer-for-sale (OFS), meaning the company will not receive any proceeds, with funds going to selling shareholders. Anthem Biosciences specializes in complex fermentation-based Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and offers fully integrated drug discovery, development, and manufacturing services.

Image not found.Bajaj Finance conducts cyber security awareness drive in Ahmedabad - DeshGujarat

Published Date: Sat, 12 Jul 2025

Bajaj Finance Ltd., a private sector NBFC, conducted a cyber fraud awareness program in Ahmedabad titled "Knockout Digital Fraud." The program aims to educate digital users about various financial threats and best practices for safeguarding their finances, aligning with the Reserve Bank of India's 2024 guidelines on fraud risk management. The initiative focuses on raising awareness about common scams, such as fake social media accounts, impersonation of financial institutions, and unrealistic investment schemes. Ahmedabad Cyber Police Range Inspector Miral Boricha urged the public to be vigilant against such schemes and to report any incidents immediately to the authorities. Bajaj Finance emphasized the importance of consumer financial safety and highlighted its ongoing efforts to provide online and offline advisories on cyber safety. The company encourages citizens to stay informed and take proactive steps to protect themselves from digital fraud.
