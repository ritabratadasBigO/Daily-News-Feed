NICE Actimize Wins 2024 Datos Insights Fraud & AML Impact Award for Best AML Transaction Monitoring Innovation - Business Wire

Published Date: Tue, 08 Oct 2024

NICE Actimize has been named the winner of the 2024 Datos Insights Fraud & AML Impact Award for Best AML Transaction Monitoring Innovation. The award recognizes NICE Actimize's advanced Embedded AML AI, which uses advanced AI and machine learning techniques to detect anomalies, uncover hidden relationships, and prioritize alerts. The Embedded AML AI improves the accuracy and efficiency of AML operations, leading to better risk management, reduced costs, and enhanced regulatory compliance. Organized crime is increasingly using scams, automated bots, and synthetic identities to commit financial crimes, making it essential for financial institutions to improve their AML programs. NICE Actimize's solution helps financial institutions manage costs more carefully and find ways to improve operational efficiency and resource utilization. The company's CEO, Craig Costigan, stated that the right use of nextgeneration technology can increase efficiency and effectiveness within AML programs, and thanked Datos Insights for recognizing their innovation.
