Image not found.Life & Health Insurance Made Affordable: What the GST Reform Means for You - Mid-day

Published Date: Mon, 06 Oct 2025

The Indian government has exempted individual life and health insurance premiums from Goods and Services Tax (GST) effective September 22, 2025. This move aims to make insurance more affordable and transparent for policyholders by reducing the overall cost of premiums. Previously, a 18% GST was levied on these premiums, meaning a policy with a base premium of Rs. 50,000 would cost Rs. 59,000. While the GST exemption will lead to lower premiums, the reduction may not be the full 18% due to input tax credit (ITC) adjustments. The actual savings will vary depending on the insurer's cost structures and previous ITC benefits factored into premiums. This reform is expected to improve affordability and awareness in the insurance sector, encouraging more individuals and families to secure their financial future.
