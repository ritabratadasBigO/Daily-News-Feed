Image not found.FICO Report: UK Credit Card Spending Patterns Shift as Veteran Cardholders Show Concerning Delinquency Trends - StockTitan

Published Date: Wed, 18 Dec 2024

FICO's UK Credit Card Market Report for October 2024 reveals mixed trends in consumer credit behavior. Average credit card sales dropped 5.5% month-on-month to $790, but remained 1% higher than October 2023. Average balances decreased 0.9% to $1,815, following typical pre-Christmas patterns. Payment behavior showed an unusual trend, with the percentage of balance paid increasing marginally in September and October to 37.2%, contrary to typical seasonal patterns. Veteran cardholders (5+ years) show the highest delinquent balances for two or three missed payments, despite having higher credit limits. The report highlights the need for risk managers to review limit management and collections contact strategies, particularly for veteran customers who may be experiencing financial stress.

Delhi-Based NBFC Exposed As Key Player In Fairplay Betting App's 4,500 Crore Money Laundering Scheme - Free Press Journal

Published Date: Wed, 18 Dec 2024

The Enforcement Directorate (ED) has uncovered a sophisticated financial network operated by the betting platform Fairplay, which was designed to conceal illegal activities. The network, facilitated by a Delhi-based nonbanking financial company (NBFC), allegedly laundered over Rs 4,500 crore. The NBFC created complex mechanisms to layer illicit funds, routing them through dummy bank accounts and entities across India, and enabled the transfer of funds to overseas destinations. The NBFC also exploited non-KYC account holders and non-whitelisted entities to conduct transactions without regulatory oversight. The investigation found over 2,000 sham bank accounts linked to the NBFC, used to circulate funds within the Fairplay ecosystem, and exploited financial services like DMT and BBPS to mask the origins and destinations of illicit funds. The ED's findings raise concerns about regulatory loopholes in the financial ecosystem and the need for stricter measures to prevent money laundering and illegal activities.
